About the Prizes
The winning stories from the 2021 Showcase will be published in Solarpunk Magazine, " Solarpunk Society Magazine, Shoreline of Infinity Science-Fiction Magazine, by the Rapid Transition Alliance, and in Extinction Rebellion publications. All winning stories are currently being illustrated by a team of artists spread across the globe. The artwork will be published on this website once complete.

Our other 2021 prizes included full scholarships to Terra.do (the world’s first online global climate school), in-person workshops by Taiwanese sustainable design education enterprise TOTES & TEES, interviews and podcast versions of the stories by Solarpunk Magazine, interviews with Ecofictology vlogger Lovis Geier, 1-to-1 mentorship with eco-writer and poet Helen Moore, animal adoption kits, plant seeds, Solarpunk Magazine annual subscriptions, certificates, and of course - books!

The winning adult stories are also being adapted into virtual drama workshops by Hamburg-based sociodrama group, Dandelion Spaces. These workshops are free and open to the public.
